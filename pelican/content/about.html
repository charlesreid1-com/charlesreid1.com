Resume

Charles Reid is a chemical engineer who earned his Ph.D. simulating turbulent multiphase combustion using a massively parallel computational fluid dynamics code running on national lab supercomputers.

Charles completed a postdoc at Lawrence Livermore National Lab, and has worked as a chemical engineer in the oil and gas industry. Charles also taught computer science and mathematics at Seattle College.

Charles now works as a software and data engineer at the Lab for Data Intensive Biology, the laboratory of Professor Titus Brown, at the University of California Davis.
