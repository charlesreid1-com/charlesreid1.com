The Levels of Edit System

Doxuments uses a Levels of Edit system to classify edits, based on the Jet Propulsion Laboratory's own Levels of Edit system. Doxuments offers four levels of edit, with each level focusing on a different aspect of the document.

Edit Level Name Description
Level 4 Compositing and Layout Edits Editing of layout and arrangement of text, figures, tables, etc. for final printing.
Level 3 Copyedits Editing to check for spelling, grammar, punctuation errors, consistency issues, and adherence to style guide.
Level 2 Mathematics and Quantitative Edits Editing of mathematical equations, quantitative references, inferences, and qualitative trends to ensure consistency and clarity.
Level 1 Logical and Technical Edits Editing to ensure correct and consistent technical details, logical idea development, and strong arguments.
